TopazEarth Rules

Follow these rules to ensure a fun experience for everyone.

*Repeat offenders will face harsher punishment.

*Fines may be issued as punishment.

  • Be respectful and use common sense.
  • No hacked clients, exploits, or unfair modifications.
  • Keep chat clean and friendly.
  • You must be at least 13 years old to play.
  • Avoid excessive or weird land claims.
  • PvP is allowed only in designated areas or with consent.
  • Town and nation names should be somewhat realistic.
  • Griefing is never allowed.
  • Don’t steal from town or nation banks.
  • Don’t kill or take mobs/pets from other peoples claimed areas.
  • Do not advertise unrelated content or servers.
  • No gold farms or macro/bot farms.
  • Lag machines and intentional lagging are forbidden.
  • Limit of 1 alt account per player.
  • Do not provoke others into breaking rules.
  • No impersonating staff or other players.
  • Report rule-breaking or bugs to staff.
  • Don't deface terrain without reason - Keep the world beautiful!
  • Don't deface or extend shorelines without reason.
  • Staff have final say.
